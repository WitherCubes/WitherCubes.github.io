A Letter to God Class Notes 4

June 21, 2021 - By Sanjay Pavan

Questions:

1) What did Lencho hope for?

Ans: Lencho hoped for rain that will give him a good harvest.

2) Why did Lencho say that the raindrops were “new coins”?

Ans: He said raindrops as new coins because a rain gives good harvest which helps him earn more coins.

3) How did the rain change? What happened to Lencho’s field?

Ans: The rain slowly changed as a hailstorm, and his crops were completely destroyed.

4) What were Lencho’s feelings when the rain stopped?

Ans: After the hailstorm, Lencho felt very sad as all of his crops were totally destroyed.

5) Who and what did Lencho had faith in? What did Lencho do?

Ans: Lencho hoped for a good rain and had faith in God and started writing a letter to god to express his worries.

6) Who read the letter?

Ans: The letter was read by the postmaster who was a firendly and pleasant person.

7) What did the postmaster do then?

Ans: First the postmaster broke out laughing and immediately turned serious and had an idea to reply to the letter.
