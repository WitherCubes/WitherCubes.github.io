Fog Mind Map

June 25, 2021

Poet

  • Carl Agust Sandburg

Theme

  • First, there is the theme of nature which Sandburg explores through the medium of fog.

Poetic Devices

Metaphor

  • Fog is compared to the little cat (the little cat’s feet).

Enjambment

  • When a sentence continues to the next line (It sits looking…. then moves on).

Personification

  • Fog has been personified - Fog comes, it sits.

Poem

  • The poem compares the fog with a cat’s walk which suits ideally.
  • The fog comes like a cat’s feet and disappeares in the similar manner.
