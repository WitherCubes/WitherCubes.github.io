Dust Of Snow Mind Map

June 23, 2021

Poet

  • Robert Frost

Theme

  • The poet feels that small and unimportant changes can make great changes in our future.

Poetic Devices

Assonens

  • The prominance of vowels throughout the line (shook down on me).

Irony

  • Rued
  • Hemlock

Enjambment

  • The way a crow shook down on me.

Alliteration

  • Saved some
  • Has heart

Poem

  • The poet tries to convey that small unimportant changes could change our life.
  • Those small changes are not only optimistic but inauspicious events too.
  • The poet’s mood was changed by just a crow shooking down snow from the hemlock tree
