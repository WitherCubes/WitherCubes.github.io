Questions:

1) Why was Mrs. Pumphrey worried about Tricki?

Ans: Mrs. Pumphrey was worried because Tricki was not able to eat anything, vomitting what it had eaten and had to hospitalized.

2) What does she do to help him? Is she wise in this?

Ans: Mrs. Pumphrey called the doctor Mr. Herriot immediately and that was a wise decision and then the doctor suggested that Tricki should be hospitalized.

3) What does ‘I’ refer to this story?

Ans: In this story ‘I’ refers to the veterinary doctor Mr. Herriot.
