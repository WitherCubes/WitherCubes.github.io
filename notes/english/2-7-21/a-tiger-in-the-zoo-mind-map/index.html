Poem

  • The poet compares the same tiger when it is present in the zoo and present in the jungle.
  • The poet says the tiger inside the cage can walk only few quite steps with his vivid stripes.
  • He then says that the tiger actually should be crawling in the long grass and hunting the plump deer near the waterhole and adds that it supposed to be scaring the people in the village near the jungle with its’s White Fsngs and claws.
  • But instead the tiger was in the cage hearing the last voice of the patrolling cars and lying down and staring at the stars.
