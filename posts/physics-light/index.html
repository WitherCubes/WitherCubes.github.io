Light

Optics

It’s a branch of physics which describes about phenomenon and laws associated with generation and propagation of light, its interaction with the matter called optics.

Optics are classified into two types

  • Ray Optics
  • Physical Optics

Ray Optics

It treats the propagation of light in terms of rays and is valid if only the wavelength of the light is much smaller than the size of the obstacle.

Ray of light

It is a direction of light in which it travels. a = size of obstacle λ = wave length of light

a » λ

Beam of light

A bundle or bunch of light rays is called beam of light.

Parallel beam of light

All the rays move parallel to each other.

Convergent beam of light

All the rays move towards a single point.

Divergent beam of light

All the rays emerge from a single point.

Production of electromagnetic waves

  • These waves are produced by oscillating charges which produces mutually perpendicular oscillating electric and magnetic fields.
  • These waves are transverse waves in nature.
  • The speed of an electromagnetic wave doesn’t depend on its frequency and wave length.
